About

Hey there, I’m the Mark, of Mark and Christine, based in Asheville. This page is for sharing small, interactive web apps with you and others out there.

This is not something I’ve ever done, the closest I’ve come to making a web page was with Weebly. It seems now that just about anyone can make, store, and serve an app to anyone in the Universe for the cost of an LLM subscription, an old laptop and $10 for the domain. I followed the Grok Build->Github->Vercel->Namecheap path- pretty easy, and that’s the magic!

You can just do things!
